Welcome to Joy’s Auto Clinic

Fast and Dependable

For over five decades now our family owned and operated auto repair shop, continues to give Canyon County and the surrounding areas honest, trustworthy, dependable services with fair prices. At Joy’s Auto Clinic, we know vehicles and complete all repairs and installations promptly.

Complete Auto Repair & Air Conditioning Service

We gladly accept all makes and models of vehicles, providing everything from inspections and brake repairs, to tune-ups. Although, we specialize in air conditioning repairs and maintenance, our highly qualified technicians can repair or install any type of car parts your vehicle may require.

Expert Diagnostics!

Fully licensed, experienced and professional, you can count on Joy’s Auto Clinic for accurate diagnostics, saving you time and money!
